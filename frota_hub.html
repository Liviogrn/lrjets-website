Overview

LR Jets operates a curated fleet to match every mission: Light Jets such as the Embraer Phenom 300 and Citation CJ4 for agile regional hops; Midsize Jets including the Hawker 900XP and Gulfstream G150 for coast-to-coast comfort; and Ultra-Long-Range/Heavy Jets like the Gulfstream G650, Bombardier Global series, and Dassault Falcon 8X for intercontinental travel. Our operational network is anchored by hubs that provide rapid access across the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, and Latin America.

Paired with professional crews, meticulous maintenance, and discreet concierge support, LR Jets delivers seamless journeys from city center to sky—and beyond.
