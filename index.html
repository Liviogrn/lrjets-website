Welcome to LR Jets - Executive Aviation

LR Jets - Executive Aviation was born from a vision to redefine private air travel in Brazil and beyond. Founded with the commitment to excellence, safety, and discretion, the company was established to serve executives, entrepreneurs, and discerning travelers who value both their time and their comfort. Our story is one of ambition and dedication, built on the desire to deliver an unparalleled flying experience.

From the very beginning, LR Jets has focused on combining cutting-edge aviation technology with world-class service standards. Our carefully selected fleet of modern jets ensures flexibility for short, regional trips as well as intercontinental journeys. Each detail of the operation is designed to provide efficiency, comfort, and complete peace of mind, while maintaining the highest levels of safety and professionalism.

Our mission is to elevate executive aviation to new heights, offering clients not just flights, but tailored solutions for their travel needs. Looking to the future, LR Jets strives to expand its international presence, integrate sustainable aviation practices, and continuously enhance the client experience. We are more than an airline — we are your trusted partner in executive travel.
